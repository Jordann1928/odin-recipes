Banana Pudding

Home Banana Pudding Ham Mac & Cheese

This is banana pudding, a delicious dessert featuring vanilla pudding, sliced banana and Nilla Wafers. This dish is best served cold and is an excellent dessert for holiday occasions. To make Banana pudding, you will need:

  • Three sliced bananas
  • Nilla Wafers
  • Condensed milk (14 oz.)
  • Vanilla extract (8 oz.)
  • One box vanilla pudding mix (14 oz.)
  • 8 oz. Cool Whip
  • 1 Tablespoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Combine vanilla pudding mix, vanilla extract, condensed milk and Cool Whip in a large bowl.
  2. Line a 5/14 pan with banana slices and Nilla Wafers.
  3. Pour pudding mix into pan and top with additional Nilla Wafers.
  4. Chill in fridge for two hours. Serve and enjoy!
