No thanksgiving feast is complete without ham! This tender, delicious meat will grab the attention of all of your guests. Want to know the best part? It's super easy to make! See below for isntructions and a list of ingredients.

  • One 10 pound ham
  • Pineapple slices
  • Brown sugar
  • 8 0z. butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F. Melt butter on stove and add brown sugar.
  2. Coat ham with melted butter and brown sugar and stick pineapple slices on ham.
  3. Heat for two hours or until internal temperature reaches 160F. Let cool, serve and enjoy!

And there you have it! Making a Thanksgiving staple is that simple. We hope you enjoy this recipe, and we're sure your guests will too!
