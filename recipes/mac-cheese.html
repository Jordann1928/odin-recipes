Mac & Cheese

Home Banana Pudding Ham Mac & Cheese

Here it is - the staple of Thanksgiving sides. Your guests will love this baked mac & cheese recipe! This is a very simple recipe to make. The recipe calls for:

  • 1/2 cup mild cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup meunster cheese
  • 1/2 cup sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 8 oz. half & half
  • 1 tablespoon salt and pepper
  • 1 pound macaroni

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F and boil macaroni until tender
  2. Grate cheese and combine in large bowl.
  3. Once macaroni has boiled, add 3/4 of cheese to macaroni and add eggs, half & half and salt and pepper.
  4. Place macaroni in baking dish. Top with remaining cheese and bake for 35 minutes uncovered.
  5. Remove, let cool and enjoy!
